Section A

Enquiry module

Critical A1 — "Qualified as Referral" gate
Missing entirely. Add qualified_as_referral, qualified_date, qualified_remarks to enquiries table. New "Mark as Qualified" button. "Create Patient" button only appears after qualification. Status flow: New → In Progress → Qualified → Converted → Not Proceeding.
Critical A2 — Link enquiry → patient
No enquiry_id on patients. EnquiryController::convert() creates a Case Manager then stops. Add enquiry_id FK to patients, "Create Patient" button on qualified enquiry, pre-fill form from enquiry data.
Medium A3 — Multiple enquirer contacts with roles
New table enquiry_contacts (enquiry_id, name, role, email, phone). Roles: Case Manager / Lead Professional / Other. Dynamic ADD ANOTHER rows on forms.
Medium A4 — Four follow-up slots
Excel has 4 follow-up date columns. Restructure enquiry show page to display communications as numbered follow-ups (date + remarks each).

Medium B3 — Auto status transitions
Make patient status transitions automatic on trigger events. See trigger table above.

Suggested improvements

Improvement 1
Enforce a linear status progression with auto-transitions
Make patient status transitions automatic on key events. Block backwards jumps. Prevents staff from skipping steps.
Improvement 2
Pre-fill patient form from enquiry data
When "Create Patient" is clicked from a qualified enquiry, auto-populate company, case manager, referral date, client location, and referrer contacts. Eliminates double data entry.
Improvement 3
Single "Patient Journey" timeline view
Chronological activity feed on the patient page: enquiry → qualified → patient created → communications → assessment → cost estimation → funding → associate allocated → invoices → case notes → discharge. Single-screen audit trail.
Improvement 4
"Quick Actions" panel — show only the next logical step
Context-aware panel on the patient page surfacing only the next action based on current status. Makes the workflow self-documenting and prevents skipped steps.
Improvement 5
Document enforcement at three critical gates
(1) Cannot mark "Funding Approved" without approval document — BR-C1. (2) Cannot mark VTA invoice "Sent" without document — BR-F6. (3) Cannot mark Assessment "Report Sent" without uploading the report.
Improvement 6
Funding balance live on patient page
FundingBalanceService already computes the balance — surface it on the patient page. Show approved amount, invoiced to date, remaining balance, and a progress bar.
Improvement 7
Associate allocation with automatic activity log
When an associate is allocated, auto-create a communication log entry and show it on the patient timeline. Sets up for future email notification.
Improvement 8
Email intake — tag emails to records
Allow each email to be tagged to a patient, enquiry, VTA invoice, or funding cycle. Makes the Emails nav section useful — all emails related to a patient visible from the patient page.
Improvement 9
Reports section — 4 basic reports to start
Active patients by status · Funding balance summary · Invoice ageing · Associate activity. Built as pre-written queries — no complex engine needed.
Improvement 10
Correct CLAUDE.md and project path
Actual project is at C:\xampp\htdocs\VTA_NEW\vta-portal not C:\xampp\htdocs\vta-portal. Also document: enquiry_id FK on patients, new Assessment table, Qualified as Referral status, 5 dashboard widgets.
